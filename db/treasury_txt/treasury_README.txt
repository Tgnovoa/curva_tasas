Notes
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Yields on actively traded non-inflation-indexed issues adjusted to    
constant maturities. The 30-year Treasury constant maturity series was
discontinued on February 18, 2002, and reintroduced on February 9,    
2006.                                                                 
For further information regarding treasury constant maturity data,    
please refer to                                                       
http://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/h15/current/h15.pdf and
